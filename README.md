# Bunge (bunge)
Bunge Global SA is a multinational agribusiness and food company connecting farmers to consumers by sourcing, processing, and supplying oilseed and grain products. Operates across grain origination, oilseed processing, refined and specialty oils, milling, sugarcane and biofuel production, and fertilizer sales.
